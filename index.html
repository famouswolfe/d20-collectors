D20 Collectors

Instructions:

  • A random number will be chosen at the start of the game.
  • There are four d20s below. By clicking on a d20, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
  • You win the game by matching your total score to the randomly generated number. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.
  • Click on a d20 to discover its value.
  • May the odds be ever in your favor!
